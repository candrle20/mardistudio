## Risks & Mitigation

### Top Risks

#### 1. AI Quality Not Good Enough
**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** High  
**Mitigation:** Train on 500+ scans, human QA, A/B testing, "Artist Touch-Up" service ($100)

#### 2. Print Defects
**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** High  
**Mitigation:** Rigorous preflight (18+ checks), test orders, defect feedback loop, hard proofs ($50)

#### 3. Slow Artist Recruitment
**Probability:** High | **Impact:** Medium  
**Mitigation:** $500 advance, success stories (artist earning $3K/month), art school partnerships

#### 4. High CAC, Low LTV
**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** High  
**Mitigation:** Optimize ads (10+ creatives), referral program (viral > 0.5), reorder incentives (10% off)

#### 5. GPU Costs Spiral
**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** High  
**Mitigation:** Spot instances (50% savings), caching (10% duplicate requests), batching, distillation
